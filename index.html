About us

Given the tough decisions taken by the Union and state governments on banning the use of plastics and the day-to-day damage to the environment, every citizen should come forward to save the environment.

Inspired by this, we started our company, Girnari Datta Enterprises on 24th August, 2020. We have started production of different designs and sizes of imported shopping bags and pouches of different sizes in imported food grade paper for essentials. Consumer demand for your goods is increasing day by day due to the reliable and quality products that we are making.

So let's go one step further to save the country's environment by purchasing the paper bags and pouches you need. Thanks!

Features

Eco-friendly

The eco friendly bags are mostly made materials that are easy to get decomposed by the bacteria and other microorganisms.

Preservation

These kraft paper bags are excellent for packing food (even hot) that will stay fresh till you eat. The reason for this is they have air pores that help in circulating air

Save Future

They are extracted from a solution that is unbleached and recycled from brown paper so that crucial natural resources and greenhouse emissions can be reduced.
